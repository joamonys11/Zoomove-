## 🕹️ Gameplay

In **Zoomove**, the player controls a rolling object using the phone’s motion (tilt controls):

* 📲 Tilt your phone **left or right** to move
* 🪙 **Collect coins** to increase your score
* 💣 **Avoid bombs** that can end your run
* ⚠️ Watch out for **debuff items** that negatively affect gameplay

The goal is simple: **survive longer and collect as many coins as possible!**
